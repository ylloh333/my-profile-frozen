⛄️OLAF⛄️

🥕Hi, I'm Olaf and I like warm hugs!❄️


My Cutie Pic

⛄️Who Am I?⛄️

Loves: Warm hugs, summer, friendship, helping others
Dislikes: Melting, danger to my friends

Habits:
Singing about summer
Asking deep, philosophical questions (like "What's the color of wind?")

IN SUMMER~🌞

⛄️Olaf's Day⛄️

Morning: I woke up, even though I remembered I don't really sleep. I cheerfully said hello to the sun, giving it my warmest greeting.😗

Noon: I tried to sunbathe and felt a little drippy. Thankfully, Sven saw me and moved me to a shady spot. We shared a moment, and a carrot.🥕

Afternoon: I joined Anna and Elsa for snow angels. Mine looked... unique.✨

Evening: As the night fell, I sat with everyone by the fireplace. I stayed a bit farther back because, you know, heat. I hummed along to Kristoff's guitar, thinking of summer.🌞

Night: Gazing up at the Northern Lights, I pondered what it's like to dream, staring at the shimmering sky until the wee hours💤
